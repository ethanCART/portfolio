Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies and pasttimes at the moment is drawing. At the moment, all of my artwork is done traditionally, or through paper while I save up funds to get a drawing tablet and switch to digital art.

I've found that personally for me, drawing is a great way to relieve stress and create something I enjoy, and being able to improve on it whenever I'd like. I do not plan to make drawing become anything more serious than a hobby, though I have consiered honing my skills and potentially sell commissions for a low, affordable price.

Another less common hobby I have that I commonly go to in order to relieve stress is editing. I find video editing and video production in general to be a very fun and rewarding task, as I'm able to create something I'm proud of, and think of ways to improve it further via editing.

Unlike drawing, I fully intend to make video and image editing part of my career, and work under an employer for a reasonable paygrade. I find it fun, and not as difficult as some make it out to be.
